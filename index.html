Phase 5 · Architecture

How IAM, IGA & PAM connect
to your ERP landscape

A single identity layer sits across all your ERP systems — governing who gets in, what they can do, and recording everything that matters.

🔐
IAM — Identity & Access Management
The front door. Authenticates every user via SSO and MFA before they reach any ERP system. Enforces context-aware access policies.
⚙️
IGA — Identity Governance
The rules engine. Enforces SoD rules, manages the joiner-mover-leaver lifecycle, and generates audit evidence automatically.
🛡️
PAM — Privileged Access Management
The vault. Controls and records access to high-risk ERP actions — payroll runs, bank changes, depreciation — with session recording and dual approval.
